# Podcast Vision

This podcast documents real engineering work: decision tradeoffs, architecture choices, and lessons learned while shipping software.

## Why It Exists

- Share practical, implementation-level insights instead of generic advice
- Discuss how product thinking changes technical decisions
- Help engineers grow from feature delivery to systems ownership

## Typical Topics

- Frontend architecture at scale
- Design system governance and component quality
- Performance and accessibility in real product timelines
- Technical communication and leadership in engineering teams
