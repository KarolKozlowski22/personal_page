# Podcast Vision

**“Rozmowy z Kozłem”** is currently an interdisciplinary podcast created by young people for everyone.

In a time of chaos, uncertainty, and fast AI adoption, its mission is to inspire a broad audience and provide practical momentum for action.

The podcast helps listeners discover new ideas for handling everyday challenges, making better life and career decisions, and ultimately living better.

## What you will hear in episodes

- conversations connecting technology, personal growth, and real-life challenges
- practical viewpoints and concrete examples
- inspiration for building your own path in a rapidly changing world
